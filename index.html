FAIRFOX ROLLER DERBY

ESTABLISHED IN 2014

FairFox Roller Derby is an all-women's flat-track recreational roller derby league based in Northern Virginia.

As a recreational league, FairFox accommodates fresh meat, veteran skaters, and skaters of all levels of experience in between.

We welcome skates of all shapes, sizes, ages, and ability.

SKATE WITH US

Contact us to skate! We are always happy to see new faces.

Our weekly practices are held on Monday night from 8:15 to 9:45 PM in Fairfax, Virginia.

All skaters are required to have the following gear:

  • quad skates
  • knee pads
  • elbow pads
  • wrist guards
  • helmet
  • mouth guard

Not sure what or where to buy? Have commitment issues? No worries--loaner gear is available for rent at $5 per practice and we are also available to answer any questions. Please first check with us to make sure your size is available. You must bring your own mouthguard.

Visiting from out of town? Our drop-in skater fee is $5 per practice.
